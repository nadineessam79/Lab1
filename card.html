The cutest animal ever!

A giraffe's neck is too tall to reach the ground. As a result, it has to awkwardly spread its front legs or kneel to reach the ground for a drink of water.

  • Scientific Name: Giraffa
  • Average Length: 2.4 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 26 years
  • Habitat: They are native to Africa

Whilst it was thought that giraffes did not make any sounds, this is now known to be untrue, as giraffes bellow, snort, hiss and make flute-like sounds, as well as low pitch noises beyond the range of human hearing.
